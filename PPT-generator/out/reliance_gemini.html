03Investment Thesis: On the Cusp of Value Unlocking

Three Growth Engines Converging to Unlock Value. We initiate on Reliance with a BUY rating as the market underappreciates the impending monetization of its consumer platforms (Jio & Retail) and ascribes minimal value to its New Energy business. We believe these twin catalysts, funded by the stable O2C cash flows, will drive a significant re-rating towards our SOTP-derived target price of ₹1717.

Three Pillars of Our Investment Thesis
Consumer Platforms Nearing Monetization

Jio and Reliance Retail are dominant, cash-generative businesses poised for value unlocking via potential public listings. We anticipate a Jio IPO in H1 2026, which will crystallize value and establish a public market benchmark.

O2C: The Resilient Cash Cow

The legacy Oil-to-Chemicals segment remains a highly profitable engine. Its strong, stable cash flows provide the financial foundation to de-risk and fund the group's ambitious capex in high-growth new ventures.

New Energy: The Third Engine

The US$10 bn investment into the Giga Complex for solar, batteries, and hydrogen represents a significant long-term value creator. We see this as a largely unpriced option with the potential to transform Reliance into a global green energy leader.

A Conglomerate of Four Market-Leading Platforms
Oil-to-Chemicals (O2C)

The foundational cash engine, featuring the world's largest integrated refining and petrochemicals complex at Jamnagar. It provides stable, robust cash flows to fund new growth initiatives.

Digital Services (Jio Platforms)

India's largest digital ecosystem with over 515 million subscribers. Dominates the telecom market with over 65% of India's 5G users and is rapidly expanding its fixed wireless broadband service.

Retail (Reliance Retail Ventures)

The nation's largest and most profitable retailer, operating a vast omni-channel network. Commands leadership across key consumption categories including grocery, electronics, and apparel.

New Energy

The designated third growth engine, backed by a planned US$10 bn capex for its Giga Complex. Aims to build a fully integrated renewable energy ecosystem, from solar panels to green hydrogen.
